About Me

My journey into tech actually started with a degree in civil engineering.  During my sophomore year I took a programming course to fill an elective requirement.   I was immediately hooked.  Something about coding drew me.   But having committed to engineering I finished my degree and entered the workforce.  Years later, while taking time off to take care of my two sons, I decided to explore coding once more.

I experienced that same pull from years before:   the more I learned to code, the more I wanted to learn.   That thirst has continued to grow to this day.   The first time I built my own website I realized I found something that would also give me the freedom and versatility I was looking for in my work.  Now I get to use my organizational and analytical skills to build websites with clean, elegant code.

What I do

Code

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is accessible, easy to read, easy to collaborate, and easy to trouble-shoot.

Style
